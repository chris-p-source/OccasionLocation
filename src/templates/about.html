Helping to find great events near you.

OccasionLocation is built with two user groups in mind. In our eyes you are either a user or a host.

As a user, the site will allow you to search for great events happening in your local area, find out more details about them, book them and even query the host of the event about the little details.

As a host, you are able to upload your events onto the site to help boost attendance. Whether you run a cookery class, child care group, book club, or anything in between, we accept all types of events. Once uploaded, you are able to communicate with users and edit the details of your listing whenever you please.

Here is to connecting great hosts with great people!
