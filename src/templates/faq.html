Frequently Asked Questions
How do I make a booking?

You can make a booking by logging into your account (registering if you dont have one), searching for an event, clicking on details and then clicking book.

Do I have a limit on how many events I can book?

There is currently not a set limit on how many events you can book at once.

How do I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking by clicking on your name once logged in to open your account settings where you are able to see all of your bookings, from here they can easily be removed by clicking "Remove".

Is there a limit on how many extra people I can bring?

This is dependent on event to event. If you are concerned please get in touch with the event host.

How do i create an event as a host?

You can create an event by clicking on "Add event" on the home page. From here you can enter the details about your event and click "Publish" in order to make the listing live.

How do i change the details of my event?

The details of your event can be changed by clicking on your username, going to my events and clicking the "Change" button.

Is there a limit on how many event I can host at once?

There currently isnt a limit on the number of events you can host at once. You will be alerted however if you try to host at the same time that you are already hosting one.

Is there a limit to how many events I can host in a certain time range?

There is currently no limit to the amount of events you can host within a certain time range.

What happens if i need to cancel my event?

Events can be cancelled by clicking on your username, going to my events and clicking the "Cancel" button.
